Swerve's Famous Energon Cocktail

Swerve's Famous Energon Cocktail is bright pink and inviting, featuring hints of magnesium and a bit of iron shavings for flair.

Delve into the dynamic world of Transformers with this electrifying cocktail, Energon Essence. Inspired by the vital energy source of the Autobots and Decepticons, this drink shimmers with a mesmerizing blue hue, symbolizing the powerful Energon fuel. Its flavors are both vibrant and complex, designed to invigorate your senses and fuel your spirit for the adventures ahead.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 Kleiner Feigling
  • 2/3 Sprite
  • Drop of Pink Food Coloring
  • Spoon of Rose Colored Edible Pearl Dust

    • Instructions

    1. In a shaker, combine the Blue Curacao, vodka, gin, lime juice, simple syrup, and electric blue food coloring.
    2. Fill the shaker with ice cubes and shake vigorously until well-chilled.
    3. Strain the mixture into the highball glass filled with ice cubes.
    4. Gently top up with soda water, creating a layered effect that mimics the dynamic energy flux of Energon.
    5. Carefully place the edible silver ball on top as garnish, positioned to catch the eye like the coveted AllSpark.
    6. Serve immediately with a twist of lime peel, subtly enhancing the aroma.
