Megatron's Peace Through Tyranny Cocktail

Megatron's Peace Through Tyranny Cocktail is bright purple and more than a little sassy. Perfect for Pride month.

Megatron is the founder of the Decepticon uprising, and their most well-known and feared leader. As a young, charismatic leader forged in battle and the heritage of war, he began to believe in a grand purpose for his raceâ€”it is the Transformers' glorious destiny to rule an empire which will span the universe. The opposite of his mortal enemy Optimus Prime, he feels great contempt for other Transformers who, he feels, betray their proud heritage by demanding peace and cooperation with weaker life forms. It is the destiny of the Decepticons to bring order to the universe through conquest, though in the millions of years since coining this purpose it remains to be seen how much of his mission statement is altruistic... and how much of it is mere words built to fuel warriors to further his desire for personal power.

Megatron will attempt almost anything to achieve his goals, but his schemes are rarely in any way subtle. Much like the flavor profile of this bright cocktail, Megatron is impossible to ignore.

Ingredients

  • Mystery Vodka
  • Lemonade
  • Orange Bitters
  • Ultraviolet monster energy drink

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a cocktail mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir
  3. Strain the mixture into a chilled glass.
  4. Cheers!
